The American Mathematics Competitions (AMC)
The AMC 8 is for students grades 8 and under. It is a 25 question, 40 minute multiple choice test.
Problems generally increase in difficulty as the exam progresses. Through 2007, calculators were
permitted, but they will not be in the future. Unlike the AMC 10 and 12, no points are deducted for
wrong answers.
