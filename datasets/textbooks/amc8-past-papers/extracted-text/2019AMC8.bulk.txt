Problem 9

cans?

Problem 13 
A palindrome is a number that has the same value when read from left to right or from right to 
left. (For example, 12321 is a palindrome.) Let 𝑁 be the least three-digit integer which is not a 
palindrome but which is the sum of three distinct two-digit palindromes. What is the sum of the 
digits of 𝑁? 
 
 
Problem 14 
Isabella has 6 coupons that can be redeemed for free ice cream cones at Pete's Sweet Trea ts. In 
order to make the coupons last, she decides that she will redeem one every 10 days until she has 
used them all. She knows that Pete's is closed on Sundays, but as she circles the 6 dates on her 
calendar, she realizes that no circled date falls on a Sunday. On what day of the week does 
Isabella redeem her first coupon?
